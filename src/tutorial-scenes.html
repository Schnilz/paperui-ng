Scenes

A scene is a collection of selected Item states.

When you execute a Scene all Scene Items will be restored to the stored state.

For example a "Cooking" scene could turn on all your kitchen lights to a dimmed value of 80% except the serving cabinet with a value of 100% and switch on the kitchen radio and tune in a specific channel.

To create a Scene, you first bring your Items into the desired state. Then you head over to the Scenes page and create a new Scene. Scenes can only be created and not modified later on.

Scenes will appear in control interfaces. Some interfaces may allow you to create a new Scene on-the-fly.

Pro: Scenes are just rules, tagged as "scene", internally. You can always modify a scene from within the Rules page.
